## Boilerplate (allowed)

FourDRenderer v2.0 is a **declared** higher-dimensional rendering architecture for operating in \(\mathbb{R}^{4}\) and projecting into host-consumable 3D via Observation Modes. It extends MRS / RT4D / Engine v1 contracts. GPU pipeline completeness, deep Unreal RHI integration, and Nanite/Lumen 4D paths are **roadmap** items and are not claimed as shipped product capabilities.
