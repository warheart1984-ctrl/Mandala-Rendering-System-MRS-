## Color answers (rewrite before publish)

**What is it?**  
The contract layer for rendering where W is real — and for landing honestly in 3D hosts.

**Does the adapter do 4D?**  
No. It drinks Scene3D + lineage. Authority stays upstream.

**Is Unreal done?**  
The blend *interface* is declared. Deep RHI is the mountain still ahead.

**4–6 ms?**  
A design target we’re not pretending we’ve measured.

**Full GPU SO(4)?**  
Architecture: yes (declared). Partial BVH / prior paths: some. Full GPU SO(4) enforcement: not yet — and we say so (see evidence-bound Q6).

**SDK?**  
A declared sketch linked to the Shader ABI — not a storefront download.
