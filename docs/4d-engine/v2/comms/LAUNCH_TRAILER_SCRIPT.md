## Intent

30–45s motion piece that sells the *idea* of higher-dimensional structure becoming visible through Observation Modes — while end cards stay evidence-honest.

## Structure

| Time | Visual | VO / Text | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0–3s | Black → faint W-axis tick marks | “There is more than the slice you see.” | Abstract; no product logo spam |
| 3–10s | Hyper-box / BVH wireframe metaphor | “FourDRenderer declares how structure lives in four dimensions.” | “Declares,” not “ships” |
| 10–18s | Ray marching metaphor through 4D nodes | “Trace. Shade. Project.” | Conceptual; not claiming live Unreal RHI |
| 18–28s | Projection collapse to familiar 3D forms | “Observation Modes choose what becomes visible.” | Aligns with RFC language |
| 28–35s | Host engine silhouette (generic, not logo lockup unless licensed) | “Hosts consume the projected world — adapters stay hybrid-first.” | Avoid “seamless Unreal” |
| 35–42s | Title card | **FourDRenderer v2.0** — Higher-Dimensional Rendering Architecture (**Declared**) | Honest title |
| 42–45s | End card | Architecture index · Scorecard · Contact **TBD** | No fake URL / press email |
